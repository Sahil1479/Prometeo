Augmented & Virtual Reality

Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality is an interactive experience of a real-world environment where the objects that reside in the real world are enhanced by computer-generated perceptual information, sometimes across multiple sensory modalities, including visual, auditory, haptic, somatosensory, and olfactory. In this discussion, you will get to know various aspects of Ar-Vr in industrial fields, academia, etc.

Future of Fintech Panel

FinTech (financial technology as a buzzword) has simplified transactions. It's dissolving the barriers between service providers, allowing banking and internet companies to offer services that are practically identical. Technologies such as IoT, AI, blockchain and cloud computing are the major drivers of the FinTech domain . The beauty of FinTech is that it will come to them rather than the other way around. This event is structured so as to call out some FinTech Industry experts as part of our panel to give their esteemed knowledge and mentorship on how the FinTech domain will look like in 2030, 2040 and 2050.
