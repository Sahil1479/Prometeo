Exhibitions

Exhibitions provide us with amazing learning experiences. The ideas in the exhibition help us to understand not only the existing innovations and art but also leave us with an impression of what lies ahead of us. The Exhibitions at PROMETEO '22 are here to bring forth a similar experience for you. The face-to-face approach and physical presence are the essences of any exhibition. However, due to the current Covid scenario, keeping everyone's safety in mind; We'll be hosting a Virtual Exhibition. We hope to bring you the best experience and learnings.
